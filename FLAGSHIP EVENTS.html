FLAGSHIP EVENTS

GO KRAFT

-(PROTOTYPE DEVELOPMENT AND TESTING PROGRAMME)

The most recent student internship we provided was a GO KART prototype development and testing programme - GO KRAFT. It was a 4-day program executed in association with ASME. All the internship programmes our team conducted which were hosted in our college managed to gain a very good number of participants.

POWERSTROKE

The basic automotive workshop has been the major event of TEAM MECKARTANS since 2016. Seven editions of Powerstroke have been completed with the lively participation of more than 250 automobile enthusiasts. The event encompasses both theoretical sessions and hands-on sessions. The sessions are handled by TEAM MACARTANS expert members who have been into national tier motor sporting competitions.We have successfully completed more than seven workshops now.

DIRTRIX

The most decorated event of TEAM MECKARTANS is DIRTRIX, a motocross extravaganza conducted in 2019. It was an FMSCI-registered motorsport race organized at the national level. DIRTRIX, the biggest of its kind in Trivandrum turned out to be a remarkable event for our team.

Quad Bike Design and Manufacturing internship

OXPO 2022

Team Meckartans,the motor sporting team of SCTCE representing the college took part in "OXPO", the annual exhibition hosted by The Oxford School Trivandrum at their campus on 22nd November 2022. The team got an official invitation from the college to conduct a class on Sustainability and to exhibit the Go Kart fabricated by the team. Members of the team had a unique chance to exhibit their work in front of school students,teachers and other attendees from multiple schools and different age groups. The team had the wonderful opportunity to explain the workings of the Go-kart and the Buggy, inspiring a large number of students in the process.
