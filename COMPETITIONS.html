BAJA SAE INDIA

The Baja SAE competition originated at the University of South Carolina in 1976, under the direction of Dr. John F. Stevens. Since then, the Baja SAE Series has become a premier engineering design series for university teams. Baja SAEINDIA is an intercollegiate engineering design competition for undergraduate and graduate engineering students. The object of the competition is to simulate real-world engineering design projects and their related challenges. Each team competes to have its design accepted by a fictitious firm for the manufacturer. The students must function as a team to design, engineer, build, test, promote and compete with a vehicle within the limits of the rules. They must also generate financial support for their project and manage their educational priorities.


The vehicles used in Baja SAE racing are often similar in appearance to dune buggies. It has Static events, consisting of Technical Inspection, Cost, Design Judging, and Sales Presentation. It also has dynamic events that include: Acceleration, Hill Climb or Traction Event, Maneuverability, Specialty Events (Rock Crawl, Mud Bog, and Suspension), and an endurance race to finish the competition.

MEGA ATV CHAMPIONSHIP

Mega ATV Championship is a national-level ATV (All terrain vehicles) racing championship in India organized by “Autosports India”. It is an inter-college national-level racing championship for engineering students basically for mechanical/ automobile branches. This championship is well known for its extraordinary racing format, night endurance racing. It was Team Meckartans’s first off-road competition and was conducted in Mega ATV race track, North Goa from 11th to 14th May 2022. Teams from more than fifty colleges participated in the event.
